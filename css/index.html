About Remy

Remy or otherwise known as 'Little Chef' by Linguini. Is an artistic, passionate, creative and intuitive rat, whose dream it is to become a famous chef in Paris.

Remy's Characteristics

  1. Remy loves and appreciates good and well made food
  2. He adores Paris and the culture within it
  3. Remy also has a Powerful sense of smell
